# Uber Trip Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Description

This project showcases a Power BI dashboard designed for analyzing Uber trip data. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of key metrics and trends related to bookings, revenue, trip characteristics, vehicle usage, and time-based patterns for a specific period (June 1, 2024 - June 30, 2024).

## Dashboard Overview

The interactive dashboard is structured into three main pages:

1.  **Overview Analysis:** Presents high-level Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and summary analyses covering:
    *   Payment type distribution.
    *   Booking volume by Day vs. Night.
    *   Weekly trend of total trip distance.
    *   Breakdown of bookings and revenue by vehicle type.
    *   Most frequent pickup/dropoff locations and farthest trip details.
    *   Location-based analysis of booking volume and vehicle preference.

2.  **Time Analysis:** Focuses on temporal patterns, visualizing:
    *   Total bookings trend by the hour of the day.
    *   Total bookings trend by the day of the week.
    *   A heatmap/matrix showing booking density across hours and days.

3.  **Details:** Offers a granular view of the underlying trip data in a tabular format, allowing for detailed examination of individual trips.

## Key Metrics & KPIs

The dashboard prominently features the following KPIs for the selected period:

*   **Total Bookings:** 103.7K
*   **Total Booking Values:** $1.6M
*   **Average Booking Values:** $15.0
*   **Total Trip Distance:** 349K Miles
*   **Average Trip Distance:** 3 Miles
*   **Average Trip Time:** 16min

## Analysis Highlights

The dashboard enables users to derive insights such as:

*   Identifying the most popular payment methods (Uber Pay leads significantly).
*   Understanding the distribution of trips between daytime and nighttime.
*   Recognizing weekly patterns in trip distance and booking volume (e.g., higher activity towards the weekend).
*   Comparing the performance (bookings, revenue, distance) of different Uber vehicle types (UberX, Comfort, Black, XL, Green).
*   Pinpointing high-traffic pickup and dropoff locations (e.g., Penn Station/Madison Sq West, Upper East Side North).
*   Analyzing booking patterns across different hours of the day and days of the week.
